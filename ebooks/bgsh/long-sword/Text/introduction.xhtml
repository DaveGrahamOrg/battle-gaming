Introduction

Welcome to this quick book on how to make your own battle gaming long sword.  The instructions are written with young people in mind.  Individuals who are high school aged, and who are many middle school aged will be able to complete this project with minimal or no parent supervision.  For a younger child, the parent should lead the project. All parents should read the instructions before letting any minor (regardless of age) construct this project in order to assess their child’s competence in completing the project safely.

Padded weapon safety is dependent upon many factors including, but not limited to, materials used, construction techniques, and quality of workmanship.  Use of these guidelines alone will not guarantee the resulting padded weapon can be safely used in mock combat.  All weapons should be inspected by a knowledgeable individual and judged for safety and integrity before use in mock combat.
