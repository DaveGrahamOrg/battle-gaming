Weapon Cover Rules

Striking Surface Covers – All striking surface must be covered with a non-scratchy pliable cloth.  A referee may make case by case exceptions to this rule.

Molded Foam Striking Surface – A molded-foam striking surface without cover is acceptable if (1) it does not scratch participants, (2) passes the strike test, and (3) is approved by game referees.

Rubber Coated Striking Surface – A rubber coated (latex, neoprene, or other similar material) striking surface without a cover is acceptable if (1) it does not scratch participants, (2) passes the strike test, and (3) is approved by game referees.

Non-Striking Surface Covers – All non-striking surfaces must be covered with a durable pliable material.
