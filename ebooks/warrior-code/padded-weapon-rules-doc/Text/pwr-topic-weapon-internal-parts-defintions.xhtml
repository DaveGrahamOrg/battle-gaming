Internal Weapon Parts Definitions

core – The portion of the weapon or shield that provides strength and rigidity.

punch protector – A part that is placed over the end of a rod shaped mock weapon core to prevent the end of the core from puncturing through surrounding padding.

rip resist – A foam construct that is placed at the end of a rod shaped mock weapon core (often over a punch protector) designed to reduce ripping of the foam padding by the end of the core.

padding – All of the foam that makes up the blade(s), bludgeon(s), physical point(s), haft, guard, hinge, and pommel of a mock padded weapon.

cover – The striking surfaces of padded weapons are to be covered with non-abrasive cloth, left with exposed molded foam, or covered with a thin plastic coating. The surface may be lightly paint.
