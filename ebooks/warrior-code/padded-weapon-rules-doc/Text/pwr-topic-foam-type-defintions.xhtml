Foam Type Definitions

open cell foam – Soft compressing foam that is good when layered with closed cell foam to form shield padding as well as ax and hammer heads.

closed cell foam – Firm foam that is good for striking surfaces, non-striking surfaces and pommels.

elastic foam – Bouncy foam like that found in foam balls (Nerf, Poof, stress) that is great to use on the sharp points of weapons like sword tips, daggers, spears, javelins, etc.
