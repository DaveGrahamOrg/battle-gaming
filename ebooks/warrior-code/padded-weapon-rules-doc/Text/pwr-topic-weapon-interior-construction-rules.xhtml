Weapon Interior Construction Rules

Tape use on Striking Surface – A striking surface may contain, just under a cloth cover, up to two layers of lightweight tape (clear tape or strapping tape). It is acceptable to have smaller areas of overlap containing up to three layers.

Weapon Twisting – Any weapon that is not omin-blade in design, must not rotate when the grip is held.

Thin Plastic Reinforcement Parts Inside Non-Striking Surface – All semi-flexible thin plastic reinforcement parts, such as the base of an ax head and hook reinforcements, must be set back from the non-striking surface by a minimum of ⅜ -inch of closed cell foam.
