Weapon Mass Classes

normal – The center of the weapon damage system. A normal mass weapon must have a minimum size classification of short. Normal mass weapons include all weapons that meet normal mass requirements but fail to meet the minimum requirement for heavy mass weapons.

light –  Smaller and/or lighter than a normal mass weapon.  Light weapons do ½ the damage of their normal counterparts.  Light mass weapons include all weapons that fail to meet the minimum requirement for normal weapons, but are still considered safe for use.  All small weapons are considered light mass weapons.

heavy – A heavy mass weapon is larger and heavier than a normal mass weapon.  Heavy handheld weapons do twice the damage of their normal counterparts when swung with two hands. A heavy mass weapon must have a minimum size classification of reach.
