Ork Creature Class

Name: Orc (Ork)

Description: Originally, the ancient race of the orc-neas from Beowulf and later reworked by J.R.R. Tolkien in his Lord of the Rings Trilogy. The Orc is a hostile primitive race of savages, which has since become a common fantasy race found in Dungeons and Dragons, Warhammer as well as World of Warcraft.

Base Class: None

Clothing Required: Worn or tattered tunic.  Armor should be patchwork of mixed parts taken from various armor forms.

Experience or Training Required: None

System of Advancement: None

Weapons Classes: As per player class

Shield Classes: As per player class

Armor Classes: Up to heavy.  Very heavy and extra heavy require a level of construction and fit that is not to be found amongst Orcs.  Orc armor is the combination of scavenged parts from the battle-field.  It should often be of "shoddy" construction.

Special Equipment Needed: Green or gray face paint and rubber pointy ears

Standard Actions: As per player class.

Creature Class Actions:  None
