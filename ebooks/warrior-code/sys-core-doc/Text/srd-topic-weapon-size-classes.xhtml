Weapon Size Classes

small – Any handheld weapon less than 18 inches in length. Any projectile less than 12 inches in length.

short – Any handheld weapon at least 18 inches in length and less than 36 inches in length.  Any projectile weapon of at least 12 inches and less than 24 inches in length.

long – Any handheld weapon at least 36 inches in length and less than 48 inches in length.  Any projectile weapon at least 24 inches in length and less than 48 inches in length.

reach – Any weapon (handheld or projectile) at least 48 inches and less than 60 inches in length. 

great – Any weapon handheld or projectile at least 60 inches.
