Target Zone Rules

Face Strikes – No weapon shall target or strike an unprotected face. The target player must wear adequate protection to prevent face injury before face target subzone can be targeted and struck during combat.

Head Strikes – No handheld weapon shall strike an unprotected upper head. The target player must wear adequate helmet protection to prevent head injury before upper head target subzone can be struck during combat.

Head Projectiles – No projectile weapon shall target an unprotected head unless referees have deemed the projectile adequately padded to prevent injury.

Neck Strikes – No weapon shall strike an unprotected neck.  Even when neck protection is provided, no player should ever intentionally strike at another player’s neck.
